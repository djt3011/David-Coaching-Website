Training Programs

One-on-One Private Sessions

Train directly with coach Taylor for the ultimate soccer coaching experience. Not only do players get a higher quantity of reps, but they get the best quality reps. Coach Taylor has the knowledge and skills to replicate appropriately challenging scenarios for players at their level. For example, receiving a pass at the correct time and place can make a huge difference in one's quality of practice. Coach Taylor customizes sessions specifically for the individual based on their personal goals and as part of a larger customized plan.

Semi-Private Sessions

Pair up with a friend or two, and join Coach Taylor for a highly customized training session. You can expect personalized demonstrations, focused attention, and constant feedback. Coach Taylor knows when it is appropriate to guide training with a partner, and when to step in for some personal reps. This method of training also enables players to practice more complex game situations involving several people.

Small Group Sessions

Picture a small group break out session at a large team practice. Players work together on technique or strategy with the full attention of the coach. Coach Taylor uses his extensive knowledge of team situations to design appropriately challenging drills. As with semi-private sessions, Coach Taylor knows when to pull a player out of the small group for individualized instruction.

Coach Taylor

Coach Taylor grew up playing in the London FA Youth leagues. Under the mentorship of ex-Arsenal midfielder Dave Price, Taylor learned everything you need to know about the fundamentals of soccer. He gained unique insights about technique and strategy from an elite player. Taylor continued his playing career through college, repesenting Loughborough University, a BUCS Premier North Division team. Upon moving to the States, Taylor continued playing in Arizona Soccer Leagues but shifted his focus to coaching. He spent 3 years as the varsity coach of McClintock High School and later went on to coach his own kids at Sereno Soccer Club, RSL-AZ North, and YNSL. Taylor also teaches in the Tempe Union High School district. Through his years of experience, he has mastered the art of differentiating instruction and making learning meaningful. Taylor brings his extensive knowledge to the next generation of players. He helps players enhance focus through motivation and high expectations. He builds players' knowledge through feedback, questioning, and analysis. He builds skill by breaking down correct technique and ensuring correct repitition. With hard work and focus, it's time to take your game to a whole new level!
